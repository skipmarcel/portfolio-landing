Sailing

Sailing has been in my life since I was pretty young. My stepfather had a Hobie Cat. He would take my mother and I sailing in Mission Bay. Although the sailing bug didn't really take hold until I was an adult living on Catalina Island working as a maintenance man at Catalina Island Camps A friend had bought a trimaran that I helped bring back to life. We sailed that boat in some of the most beautiful water San Diego has to offer. I was hooked from then on. I now have my own monohull here in the Pacific NW and cruise the San Juan Islands as often as I can.
